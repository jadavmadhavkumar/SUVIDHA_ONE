### Visual Style Guidelines

- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: #1A3C8F (Deep Blue)
  - Accent: #FF6600 (Saffron)
  - Success: #217346 (Green)
  - Background: White/Light Grey

- **Animation Style**:
  - Flat design with subtle shadows
  - Smooth transitions (ease-in-out)
  - Icon-based storytelling
  - Indian design elements (rangoli patterns subtly in backgrounds)

- **Typography**:
  - English: Noto Sans Bold
  - Hindi: Noto Sans Devanagari Bold
  - Large, readable text on screen

### Recommended Tools for Production

1. **Animated Video Platforms**:
   - Vyond (GoAnimate)
   - Animaker
   - Powtoon
   - Canva Video
   - Adobe After Effects (professional)

2. **Voiceover**:
   - Professional Hindi+English bilingual voice
   - Warm, trustworthy tone

3. **Music**:
   - Upbeat but not distracting
   - Indian instrumental elements welcome
   - Royalty-free options from Epidemic Sound / Artlist
